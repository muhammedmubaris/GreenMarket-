The Core Philosophy

Treating waste not as a sanitation problem, but as a logistics and supply chain optimization problem.

Radical Transparency

Establishing an undeniable "source of truth" via open APIs, allowing citizens to track vectors just like food delivery apps.

Proactive Maintenance

Shifting from reactive citizen complaints to automated, computer-vision generated work orders via existing CCTV/dashcam infrastructure.

Financial Incentives

Tying contractor payouts directly to automated trustless sensors (Hygiene Ledgers) and tokenizing organic waste as an actual commercial asset.

The Moduler Clean-Stack

5 independent, interoperable solutions to digitize municipal sanitation from collection to monetization.

01

Logistics Dashboard & API

GPS Telemetry IoT Load Sensors

Eliminates "phantom trips" and the secondary collection gap. A public open-API allows citizens to see exactly where the "Kuppai Vandi" (garbage van) is in real-time, functioning like Zomato for trash.

02

Automated "Black Spot" Detection

Computer Vision YOLOv8 Edge

Uses existing city CCTV feeds or public bus dashcams. An AI model detects roadside trash piles (GVPs) and automatically issues geo-tagged work orders to the nearest sanitation worker.

Trash 98%
03

Vellakkal Digital Twin

GIS Drone Photogrammetry

A 3D transparent ledger of the Vellakkal dumpsite. Drones calculate the exact volume of "Legacy Waste" processed via biomining every week, establishing undeniable proof of work and preventing financial corruption.

04

Smart-Toilet "Hygiene Ledger"

IoT Sensors Web3 Smart Contracts

Tackling the maintenance accountability problem. VOC (smell) and usage sensors log cleanliness to a Polygon ledger. Contractors only get paid if the aggregate "Hygiene Score" remains above a strict threshold.

85%
Payout Authorized
05

Hyper-Local Waste Trading

PWA Marketplace UPI Integration

A B2B Progressive Web App turning waste into a commodity. Vendors at Mattuthavani can instantly sell organic waste to biogas plants, incentivizing segregation over street dumping.

GTM Rollout Strategy

A phased approach based on impact vs hardware complexity.

1

Phase 1: Quick Wins & Trust

Months 1-4

  • Launch Hyper-Local Waste Trading PWA (Zero hardware dependencies)
  • Release Madurai Clean-Stack API with simple truck GPS nodes
2

Phase 2: Automation & Pilot

Months 5-9

  • Automated Black Spot Detection integrated heavily into city buses
  • Pilot Smart-Toilet Hygiene Ledger in 5 major bus terminals
3

Phase 3: Deep Audit Layer

Months 10-12

  • Full roll-out of Vellakkal Digital Twin to hold biomining accountable
  • IoT Load sensors attached to 100% of municipal fleet
